My Story

Hello beautiful people, my name is Meishaundra, but you can call me Mimi. I have been baking sweet treats for about 10 years and I am new to crafting, but I have a passion for creating things that make people happy. For a few years, I took a break to go to nursing school, and now I'm back to share my passion of creating sweet treats and crafts with whomever wants them! I have two beautiful daughters and a husband who are my world and my guinea pigs. I am a southern girl, so most of the things I create are Southern in nature. The name Cincerly Fay's comes from my daughters' middle names that I combined together.
Please feel free to contact me if you see something on my page that you like.
Thank you so much for the opportunity to make you smile with either a craft or a sweet treats!
